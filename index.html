A Viagem de D.Beatriz

Client: FLUP

Project Description

Lisbon, 1521 - The young Infanta D.Beatriz prepares for the journey of her life towards her union with Casa de Sabóia.

Join D.Beatriz and embark on this 49-day trip. Retrace the steps of the princess and discover key moments of life at sea; look around the ship for her missing objects and enjoy the journey from Lisbon to Savoy through a first person point-and-click game.
